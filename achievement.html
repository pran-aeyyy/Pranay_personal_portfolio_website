-- Top 30 Teams in Smart India Hackathon'23

# I was the team lead of the Team Perceptron's.

# It was a great experience to lead and manage a team.

# We were a team of 6 memebers, where each member came with a different set of skills.

# We had chosen a problem statement related to Skin Diseases. The motivation behind choosing this problem statement:

1. Skin cancer rates are on the rise, and early detection is crucial for successful treatment. Developing innovative technologies for early skin cancer detection can save lives and reduce the impact of this prevalent disease.

2. Millions worldwide suffer from the chronic skin condition psoriasis. Developing more effective and targeted treatments can significantly improve the quality of life for individuals dealing with this condition.

3. Different cultural practices and skin types may require tailored skincare approaches. Studying the impact of cultural factors on skin health and developing culturally sensitive skincare solutions can improve outcomes for diverse populations.

# We prepared our model and presentation, and pitched our idea in front of the judges. The judges were very experieced and gave us some valuable feedbacks, which we tried to implement on our model as well.

# The judges were very impressed by our idea and presentation and we got selected as one of the top 30 teams in the University Level SIH'23 Round.
